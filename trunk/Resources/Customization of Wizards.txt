Magi Customization:
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Just like in MoM, there were traits for your
wizard. Let's work it off of a 10pt. buy
system.

Economic: 2pts
   ¤Reduces the cost of towers by 20%

Charasmatic: 2pts
   ¤Reduces the cost of heroes by 20%

Sorcerer: 2pts
   ¤Reduces the cost of researching by 20%

Mana Keeper: 3pts
   ¤Gain an extra 3 mana/sec

War Magi: 6pts
   ¤Reduce the cost of casting by 50%






Also, they can purchase books. Each book will
boost their magic (Durations/Dmg/heal) by
10% per book.
